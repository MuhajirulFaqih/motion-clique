How our animations increased conversions for an online store

We created interactive product animations that enhanced user engagement and understanding, leading to a significant increase in conversion rates.

Challenge

Fashion Forward, a growing online retailer, struggled with low conversion rates despite high website traffic. Customers were visiting product pages but often left without making a purchase. The issue was that static product images and descriptions werenâ€™t fully capturing the features and benefits of the products, leaving customers uncertain about their buying decisions.

Solution

We developed a series of interactive product animations that showcased key product features, such as 360-degree views and animated product highlights. These animations allowed customers to see the products in action, providing a more dynamic and informative shopping experience. By adding movement to otherwise static pages, the animations drew attention and kept users engaged longer.

Results

25%

Increase in conversion rates within three months of launching the animations.

Higher engagement

with users spending more time on product pages and interacting with the animated elements.

Reduced bounce rates

as customers were more informed and confident in their purchase decisions.
